About Me

Hi! My name is Charlotte, I am a Third year at Northeastern University pursuing a BS in Business Administration and Data Science with a concentration in Brand Management. I am originally from Seattle, Washington, so naturally, I love hiking, dogs and specialty coffee.

On campus I am involved in several extracurriculars, including, Sigma Kappa Sorority, Women in Business, and WISE. Being involved in these organizations has provided me with a community and network of strong women who have supported me throughout my college journey and provided me with invaluable leadership experiences. I am the current president of the Sigma Kappa, Kappa Omega chapter, and I have previously held roles as the Vice President of Academic Excellence and the Activities Chair. In Women In Business I have had the pleasure of being a peer mentor for the past two semesters. This is a program where you are paired with underclassmen to be a resource for them regarding classes, co-op search, and concentration selection.

In my time at Northeastern, I have completed two Marketing internships for Proxi Co and Relish (Seattle, WA), and one co-op as a project manager at MFS Investment Management (Boston, MA). After graduation, I plan to pursue a career in a Technical industry where I hope to combine my business and Data Science roles.

I love spending my free time exploring Boston's many coffee shops and restaurants, running along the Charles River, and hanging out with friends.
